WELCOME TO NombreApp

An interactive open source software tool for rapid analysis of diagnostic and antimicrobial patterns in R (NombreAPP).

NombreAPP enables intuitive, rapid and reproducible quality of care pattern analysis of infectious patients without prior software experience. It facilitates understanding and communication of important trends, performances and patient outcome. Preprint available here: https://doi.org/10.1101/347534

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH NombreAPP:

  • Upload any CSV with data, whose Y variable consists of two categories only.
  • Select your CSV separator type. 
  • Choosing from a large number of models using your data. 
  • Observe the ROC  curve and the area under the curve (AUC) .
