WELCOME TO poli[Clasificador] 

An interactive software tool for small businesses to take their first steps in machine learning decision making, without having to hire a full team of data scientists.

poli[Clasificador] offers the possibility to train 10 of the most popular classification models, with the possibility to use cross-validation and a cost matrix, which generates an additional attraction for companies. In addition, we can also compare the classifications performed by different models on the same problem with their respective ROC curves.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH poli[Clasificador]:

  • Upload any CSV with data, whose Y variable consists of two categories only.
  • Select your CSV separator type. 
  • Choosing from a large number of models using your data. 
  • Observe the ROC  curve and the area under the curve (AUC) .
  • NEW! We also have the option of applying costs!
